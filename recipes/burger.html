Burger Recipe

Description

The classic homemade burger is the ultimate comfort food that brings together juicy ground beef, perfectly seasoned and grilled to golden perfection. This timeless American dish combines a succulent meat patty with fresh, crisp toppings, all nestled between a soft, toasted bun that promises a delightful culinary experience.

Each bite of this burger is a symphony of flavors and textures, from the smoky char of the meat to the crunch of fresh lettuce and the creamy richness of melted cheese. Whether you're cooking for a summer barbecue or a quick weeknight dinner, this burger recipe offers a delicious and satisfying meal that appeals to food lovers of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or American)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Optional: Pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat
    • In a large bowl, gently mix ground beef
    • Add salt and pepper
    • Form 4 equal-sized patties, making a small indent in the center
  2. Prepare Toppings
    • Slice onions
    • Slice tomatoes
    • Wash and dry lettuce leaves
    • Set aside all toppings
  3. Cook the Burgers
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet or grill pan
    • Cook patties 4-5 minutes per side for medium
    • Add cheese during last minute of cooking
    • Let rest for 2-3 minutes after cooking
  4. Assemble the Burger
    • Toast hamburger buns
    • Spread condiments on bottom bun
    • Place cooked patty
    • Add cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion
    • Top with second bun
    • Serve immediately
