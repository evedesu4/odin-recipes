Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish that layers rich, hearty flavors in a way that transforms simple ingredients into a culinary masterpiece. This traditional recipe combines tender pasta sheets with a robust meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a generous topping of melted mozzarella, creating a dish that is both complex and deeply satisfying.

Originating from the regions of Italy, lasagna has become a global favorite that represents the essence of home cooking. Each carefully constructed layer tells a story of tradition, with the slow-simmered tomato sauce, perfectly cooked ground beef, and delicate pasta sheets coming together to create a meal that warms both the body and soul. It's not just a dish, but a celebration of family, comfort, and the joy of shared meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 2 jars marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    • Brown ground beef in large skillet
    • Drain excess fat
    • Add marinara sauce
    • Simmer for 15-20 minutes
  2. Prepare Cheese Mixture
    • Mix ricotta cheese
    • Add eggs
    • Add parmesan
    • Season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  3. Cook Noodles
    • Boil lasagna noodles
    • Drain and lay flat
    • Prevent sticking with olive oil
  4. Layer Lasagna
    • Spread thin layer of sauce in baking dish
    • Layer noodles
    • Add meat sauce
    • Add ricotta mixture
    • Sprinkle mozzarella
    • Repeat layers
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil
    • Bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes
    • Uncover, bake 25 more minutes
    • Let rest 15 minutes before serving
